# PROMPT: Follow-Up Email Generator

Using the product context and the call summary provided, write a follow-up
email to send within 24 hours of the call.

## Email Requirements

**Tone:** Technical peer, not salesperson. Write as if one engineer is
writing to another — helpful, specific, no fluff.

**Length:** Medium. Long enough to be substantive, short enough to be read.
No bullet-point walls. Mostly prose with 1–2 structured lists maximum.

**Must include:**
1. A personalized opening that references something specific from the call
   (a pain they mentioned, a quote, a decision they're facing)
2. A recap of the 2–3 most relevant pain points they confirmed — in their words
3. A mapping of those pains to our specific product features (not generic)
4. If relevant: address any concern or objection they raised on the call
5. A single, specific, low-friction call to action (one next step, not a menu)

**Must NOT include:**
- Generic phrases like "as discussed," "per our conversation," "excited to connect"
- A full product overview or feature list
- More than one ask / next step
- Attachments listed without context

## Output Format

Provide:
1. Subject line (2–3 options, ranked)
2. Full email body
3. One-line "AE briefing note" — what the AE should know before they send it
