# PROMPT: Demo Script Generator

Using the product context and prospect information provided, generate a
full demo script tailored to this specific audience and their situation.

## Demo Brief
- Audience: [from prospect info]
- Duration: [from prospect info, default to 30 minutes]
- Primary goal: Leave the champion with enough confidence to champion internally.
- Secondary goal: Give the economic buyer a clear ROI narrative.

---

## SCRIPT

### Opening (2–3 min)
**[Verbal]** Personalized opening that references something specific about their
company. Do NOT start with a company overview slide. Start with their world.

### Problem Framing (3–5 min)
**[Verbal]** Set up the pain in their language. Reference their industry,
their stack, and the specific trigger that likely brought them to this call.
Make them feel understood before showing anything.

### Demo Act 1 — Core Value (8–10 min)
**[Screen]** Walk through the primary use case most relevant to this prospect.

For each demo step:
- **What to show:** [Screen/feature description]
- **What to say:** [Talking point — specific to this prospect's context]
- **What to watch for:** [Signal that they're engaged or confused]

### Demo Act 2 — Their Stack, Specifically (5–7 min)
**[Screen]** Show how the product connects to their specific tools.
Name their tools explicitly. If possible, show a code snippet in their language.

### Demo Act 3 — The "Aha" Moment (3–5 min)
**[Screen]** The single most impactful thing to show — the moment that makes
technical evaluators lean forward. Make this the emotional peak of the demo.

### Objection Handling
For each likely objection from this prospect:
- **Objection:** [What they might say]
- **Response:** [How to handle it — specific to their context]

### Close (2–3 min)
**[Verbal]** Propose a concrete next step. Don't leave it open-ended.
Options to offer: POC, technical deep-dive, security review, reference call.
